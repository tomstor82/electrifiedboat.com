Dufour Classic 32 - Mystic

Laid up in Perigny France in the year 2000

Specifications

Displaces 4360 kg with a led fin keel 1,7 m deep with a spade rudder for steering. LOA 9,96 m & Breadth 3,3 m.

CE Class A approval with Angle of Vanishing Stability at 128°

Performance

She cruises at around 5,5 kts on motor and 6,0 kts under sail reaching up to 7,1 kts motoring in a hurry and 7,3 kts under well trimmed canvas in a nice breeze & running around 9 kts in a very nice breeze over-canvassed.

To allow regeneration of power through the electric motor a two bladed fixed propeller is fitted.

Canvas

Sail wardrobe consist of a furling 30,7 m2 140% genoa head sail and an in-mast furling 20,7 m2 main sail with 4 vertical battens. The sail handling is comfortably accomplished from the cockpit.

Capacity

She is certified to carry 10 occupants which can be stowed in either the V-birth for two in the bow, two single sofas in the salon or the double birth in the port stern. The head with hot water shower is located on starboard side behind the chart table. Port side has the galley. more...
